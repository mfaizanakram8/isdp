Who We Are

Initiative for Social Development & Peace (ISDP) is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities by providing education, healthcare, clean water, and employment opportunities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a better world by supporting those in need through sustainable development projects, community empowerment, and humanitarian aid.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual has access to basic necessities, opportunities for personal growth, and a peaceful environment to thrive.
